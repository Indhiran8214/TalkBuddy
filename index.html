Welcome to TalkBuddy

TalkBuddy is your personal companion for seamless communication through text and speech. Whether you're typing a message or speaking naturally, TalkBuddy transforms your interaction into a smooth and efficient experience. It's designed to help you express yourself faster, easier, and smarter.

About

TalkBuddy is a web-based tool designed to assist users in converting text into speech (Text-to-Speech) and speech into text (Speech-to-Text) seamlessly.

Key features include:

  • 🔊 Real-time text-to-speech using multiple voices
  • 🎤 Speech recognition to convert your spoken words into typed text
  • 💾 Note-saving functionality for storing quick ideas or memos
  • 📱 Designed to be responsive for both desktop and mobile use
